About Us

Our Story

Bikecare360 started as a small bike service shop in Madurai, Tamil Nadu. What began as a passion project by P.S Motors grew into India's most trusted bike care brand, powered by honest product reviews and tutorials on YouTube. Today, with over 1.65 million YouTube subscribers and 1.5 lakh+ satisfied customers, we continue our mission: helping every rider keep their machine in peak condition.

We handpick every product we sell. If it doesn't meet our standards, it doesn't make the cut. From engine oils to GPS trackers, everything on Bikecare360 has been tested and approved by our team of experienced mechanics.
